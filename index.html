Problem Statement & Customer Segments

Sepsis kills millions because it is detected too late, especially in rural areas without advanced hospitals.

Customers:

  • Primary health centres
  • Small hospitals
  • Home-care providers
  • Ambulance & telemedicine services

Unique Value Proposition

A low-cost, device that monitors multiple vital signs (HR, Temp, SpO2, BP) in real time and sends alerts via app + offline LCD display. Designed for low-resource settings and can be upgraded with WBC monitoring.
